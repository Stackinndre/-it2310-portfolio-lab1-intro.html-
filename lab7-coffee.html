About Us

Hi, I'm Andre Donerson — founder, head barista, and the heart behind Java Haven.

Java Haven was born from a simple belief: that a great cup of coffee can turn any ordinary morning into something worth waking up for. What started as a passion project has grown into a community gathering place where every cup is crafted with care.

With a background in culinary arts and a deep love for specialty roasting, I source beans directly from small farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala — building relationships that go beyond transactions.

Whether you're a daily regular or stopping in for the first time, you're family here.
